How To Play:
Left Click - Movement and Knife Slash
Right Click - Fire Poison Dart
Q - healing potion
ctrl + A - Give player all abilities
9 - Give player all custom gear
P - Teleport player to Boss Room
E - Poison cloud ability
R - Smoke bomb + decoy ability
Get behind enemy and use knife stab to get a guaranteed critical hit

Objective:
Progress through the linear gated tutorial
Explore the left path and complete the arena combat -> collect the first Labyrinth Key
Explore the right path and complete the second arena combat -> collect the second Labyrinth Key
Use the shop to upgrade items and build your character!
The Labyrinth keys unlock the forward path
Complete the third arena combat
Progress to the Boss Room
Defeat the boss
